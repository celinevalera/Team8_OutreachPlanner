What is Up?

Upscale. Upskill. Uplift.

Up offers assistance with documentation and planning to make sure that outreach programs are safe and well-planned. Additionally, it aids in information dissemination, making it simpler to participate and get involved.
Up is an outreach planner project that is developed by the following Computer Engineering students of Technological Institute of the Philippines.
