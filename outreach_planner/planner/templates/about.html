About UP

Up provides aid in documenting and planning outreach programs to ensure that they are secured and well-organized by the client. It is accessible to the student volunteers who are interested and willing to participate in the outreach programs. Moreover, it helps in disseminating information about the outreach activities to the students, organized and finalized by the client, making it easier for them to be engaged and to be involved.

Up is a CPE025 Software Design outreach planner project that can be used by the Computer Engineering Department of Technological Institute of the Philippines.
